Heres a quick tip for managing projects in VIM, similar to how you can have 'projects' in Sublime.

Theres a cool little feature that comes with VIM called VIM Sessions. These are basically saved workspaces with all your tabs and what-not.

To create a session:

:mksession ~/mysession.vim

Then, to restore your old session from inside vim:

:source ~/mysession.vim

If you want to restore the session outside of vim:

$ vim -S ~/mysession.vim
